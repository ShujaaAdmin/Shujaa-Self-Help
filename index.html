About Us

We are a community-driven organization dedicated to empowering individuals and families through collective action and financial support. Our mission is to provide a secure savings platform and access to loans at friendly interest rates, fostering connection and resilience within our community.

Membership Eligibility

Any person over the age of eighteen (18) years with reliable source of income is allowed to join.The Group attracts a minimum of ten (10)) and a maximum of Five hundred (500) members.Every member is required to make a minimum weekly contribution of Ksh. 100 which is done on Sundays, latest by 2200hrs. A fine of Ksh.20 is charged upon late payment.

Loan Information

Loans are offered at a compound interest rate of 10% per month.Members can borrow based on their contribution and that of a guarantor.Non-members may borrow up to 1.5% of their guarantorâ€™s savings if the guarantor has no pending loans.

Meetings

General meetings include Annual, Monthly, and Special meetings. The Annual General Meeting is held on December 12, with all members required to attend or pay a fine of Ksh. 50 for absence without apology.
